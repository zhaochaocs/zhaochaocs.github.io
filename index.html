Bio

I'm actively seeking employment opportunities in the industry (NLP research, MLE, Data Science, etc). Please don't hesitate to reach out if you believe I would be a good fit for your team.

I'm a fifth-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, under the supervision of Prof. Snigdha Chaturvedi. My research focuses on text summarization, natural language generation, and narrative understanding.

I hold a Master's degree in Computer Science from the University of California Santa Cruz and another Master's degree in Computer Technology from Harbin Institute of Technology. I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Flight Vehicle Design and Engineering at the School of Astronautics, Harbin Institute of Technology.
