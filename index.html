Bio

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, under the supervision of Prof. Snigdha Chaturvedi. My research focuses on text summarization, natural language generation, and narrative understanding.

I obtained my Master's degree in Computer Science from the University of California Santa Cruz and another Master's degree in Computer Technology from Harbin Institute of Technology. I completed my Bachelor's degree in Flight Vehicle Design and Engineering at the School of Astronautics, Harbin Institute of Technology.
